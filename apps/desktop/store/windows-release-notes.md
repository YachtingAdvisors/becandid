# Be Candid for Windows v1.0.0

## What is Be Candid?

Be Candid is a digital accountability companion that helps you stay honest about your screen time. It runs quietly in your system tray, monitoring your activity and keeping your accountability partner informed.

## System Requirements

- Windows 10 or later (64-bit)
- 4 GB RAM minimum
- Internet connection required for sync

## Installation

1. Download `Be Candid Setup.exe` from the [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/YachtingAdvisors/becandid/releases) page.
2. Run the installer. It will install and launch automatically.
3. Sign in with your Be Candid account.
4. The app will appear in your system tray and begin monitoring.

## SmartScreen Warning (Unsigned Builds)

Windows may show a SmartScreen warning because the app is not yet code-signed. To proceed:

1. Click **More info** on the SmartScreen dialog.
2. Click **Run anyway**.

This warning will be removed once the build is signed with an EV code signing certificate.

## Features

- **Background heartbeat** -- Your partner knows the app is running, even when nothing is flagged.
- **Focus detection** -- Detects what is on screen using periodic AI-powered screenshot analysis.
- **Partner alerts** -- Your accountability partner receives notifications when concerning activity is detected.
- **System tray integration** -- Runs silently in the background with no visible window.
- **Auto-start** -- Optionally launches when you log in to Windows.

## Privacy

- **No screenshots are stored.** Images are analyzed in memory and immediately discarded.
- **No URLs are logged.** The app does not record browsing history.
- All monitoring data is encrypted in transit.
- You control what is monitored and who receives alerts.
