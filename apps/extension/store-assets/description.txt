FULL DESCRIPTION:

Be Candid helps you build awareness of your digital habits with privacy-first browsing monitoring.

HOW IT WORKS
• Tracks which websites you visit and how long you spend on them
• Automatically categorizes sites (social media, streaming, news, etc.)
• Flags or blocks distracting and harmful content
• Sends data to your Be Candid dashboard for insights and accountability

PRIVACY FIRST
• Only domain names are tracked — never full URLs, page content, or form data
• Domains are SHA-256 hashed before leaving your device
• All classification happens locally in your browser
• No screenshots, keystrokes, or screen recordings — ever

ACCOUNTABILITY FEATURES
• Share awareness summaries with a trusted accountability partner
• Partners see categories and severity levels, never raw browsing data
• Real-time alerts for high-severity browsing events
• Works with Be Candid's check-in system, journal, and focus tools

WORKS OFFLINE
• Events queue locally when you're offline
• Automatically syncs when your connection returns
• Failed uploads retry for up to 7 days

GETTING STARTED
1. Create a free account at becandid.io
2. Install this extension
3. Sign in with your Be Candid credentials
4. Browse normally — your dashboard updates in real-time

Requires a Be Candid account (free tier available).
