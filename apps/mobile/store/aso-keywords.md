# Be Candid — Android ASO Keyword Strategy

> Research-informed keyword list for Google Play Store optimization.

## Primary Keywords (High Volume)

These are broad, high-search-volume terms. Competition is fierce, but they
define the category and must appear in the title, short description, or first
paragraph of the full description.

| Keyword | Est. Volume | Competition | Where to Place |
|---------|-------------|-------------|----------------|
| accountability app | High | High | Title, short desc |
| screen time app | Very High | Very High | Full desc paragraph 1 |
| porn blocker | Very High | High | Full desc (feature list) |
| parental control | Very High | Very High | Avoid — not our positioning |
| digital wellness | High | Medium | Short desc, full desc |
| sobriety tracker | High | Medium | Full desc, keywords |
| addiction recovery app | High | Medium | Full desc paragraph 1 |
| screen time tracker | Very High | Very High | Full desc |

**Note on "parental control":** This term drives massive volume but attracts
the wrong audience and misrepresents the product. Use it only as a negative
differentiator ("Be Candid is not a parental control app — it is voluntary
accountability for adults").

## Keyword Placement Strategy

| Surface | Characters | Priority Keywords |
|---------|------------|-------------------|
| App Title | 30 max | "Be Candid — Digital Accountability" |
| Short Description | 80 max | accountability, digital wellness, values |
| Full Description | 4000 max | All primary + long-tail, natural density |
| Developer Name | — | Not keyword-stuffable |

### Density Guidelines

- Title: 1-2 primary keywords
- Short description: 2-3 keywords, one value proposition
- Full description first paragraph: 4-5 primary keywords
- Full description body: natural inclusion of long-tail and category terms
- Avoid keyword stuffing — Google penalizes unnatural repetition

## Localization Notes

Priority localization markets (in order):
1. English (US) — primary
2. English (UK/AU/CA) — spelling variants (behaviour, programme)
3. Spanish (US) — large US bilingual audience
4. Portuguese (BR) — high Android market share
5. German — strong digital wellness awareness

Translate category keywords, not just the listing. "Pornsucht" (DE),
"adiccion al juego" (ES), etc. perform well in local search.
