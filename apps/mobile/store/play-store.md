## Full Description

**Be Candid is not a spy app. It is an accountability partner that helps you live in alignment with your values.**

Most "accountability" software takes screenshots, logs every URL you visit, and sends it all to someone else. That approach is surveillance — and surveillance breeds shame, not growth. Be Candid takes a fundamentally different approach rooted in clinical research.

**What Be Candid Does**

Be Candid combines the proven frameworks behind recovery science — Stringer's journaling methodology, Motivational Interviewing, and the accountability structures of twelve-step programs — into a single, private, mobile-first experience.

You define what you want to change. Be Candid gives you the structure, the prompts, and the community to actually do it.

**Key Features**

- Conversation Coach — An AI-guided accountability conversation that meets you where you are. No lectures. No judgment. Real dialogue grounded in Motivational Interviewing techniques.

- Stringer Journaling — Structured journal prompts based on the Stringer framework, designed to surface patterns in your behavior and connect actions to values. Not a blank page — a guided process.

- Daily Check-Ins — Brief, honest pulse checks that build self-awareness over time. Track your emotional state, triggers, and momentum without the overhead of a full journal entry.

- Focus Streaks — Visual streak tracking that rewards consistency, not perfection. See your momentum build across days, weeks, and months.

- Partner Alerts — Invite a trusted accountability partner who receives alerts only when you ask for support or when a pattern suggests you might benefit from a check-in. They see momentum and mood — never your browsing history, never your screen.

- Therapist Portal — Licensed clinicians can connect with clients through Be Candid, receiving structured insights from journal entries and check-ins to make sessions more productive. Available on the Therapy tier.

- Digital Fasting — Scheduled breaks from specific apps or categories. Not a blunt blocker — a deliberate practice with intention-setting and reflection built in.

- Group Accountability — Small, private groups for people working on the same challenges. Share streaks, encourage each other, and stay connected between meetings.

**17 Rival Categories — Not Just One Problem**

Be Candid recognizes that digital struggles extend far beyond a single category. Track and build accountability across 17 rival categories including:

- Pornography and explicit content
- Gambling and sports betting
- Compulsive social media use
- Excessive gaming
- Doom scrolling and news addiction
- Online shopping and impulse spending
- Digital isolation and avoidance
- Dating app overuse
- Substance-related content seeking
- And more

You choose the categories relevant to your life. Be Candid adapts to your goals.

**Privacy-First Architecture**

Be Candid never captures screenshots of your screen. It never logs the URLs you visit. It never records your browsing history. Your accountability partner sees your momentum score and mood trends — not your private activity. This is accountability built on trust, not surveillance.

**Pricing**

- Free — Core check-ins, basic journaling, one accountability partner, and community access.
- Pro ($9.99/month) — Conversation Coach, full Stringer journaling, Focus Streaks, Digital Fasting, unlimited partners, and group accountability.
- Therapy ($19.99/month) — Everything in Pro plus Therapist Portal integration, clinical insights, and priority support.

**Built on Research, Not Fear**

Be Candid is built on decades of clinical research into behavior change. The Stringer journaling framework, Motivational Interviewing, and the accountability structures of recovery programs are not marketing buzzwords — they are the evidence-based foundation of every feature.

**Your next step is simple.** Download Be Candid, define what alignment looks like for you, and start building momentum today.
